FAQ's

What is CWay?

Cway is a digital wallet, which ensures real-time, secure and instant payment to various merchants. With Cway, you can make various payments such as utility bill payment (NEA, Khanepani, etc.), recharge mobile, pay internet bill, airlines and bus ticketing, movie ticketing, make a bank transfer (Cway to bank scan and pay to the merchants, and many more. An individual when registers and uses Cway, gains the cashback and reward points on transactions made.

Is Cway secure?

Cway is a business entity regulated by the Nepal Rastra Bank’s rules and regulations, and an ISO ceritified digital wallet. Hence, it is the safe and easy mode of payment.

How do I get my money back if I no longer want to use Cway?

With the bank transfer option available with us, you can have your amount transferred to the bank, if you wish to discontinue using Cway.

How can I withdraw money from my Cway account to a bank account?

You can withdraw Cway balance to a bank account by visiting to ‘bank transfer’ option on your mobile app or on web portal. Login to Cway>> Bank transfer>> fill the details and make withdraw.
